About

Built by a creator, for creators.

DJ Cracka is a DJ, streamer, FPV pilot, and builder. The suite started as tools for running Cracka.gg's own business — then got polished into software anyone can use, with a shared visual identity and a licensing system that works out of the box.

Same energy carries into the 3D designs and the streams: real work, real testing, and a look that's unmistakably Cracka — a randomized 5-color cyberpunk terminal, block-letter branding, and no bloat.
